FAQ/Contact Us

This is where a few FAQs go. If you still have questions that we did not answer here, please feel free to send a message!


How much is the food?
Prices are subject to change based on availability. Please see in stores for current pricing.

Can I make a reservation?
We currently do not take reservations, but we are working on a queue system to manage lines.

Is everything raw/uncooked?
You are in luck, not all our sushi is raw! We have some fried dishes and baked rolls.

Is the fish local?
Our fish is sourced locally from San Diego and Los Angeles! We get fresh shipments every moring.

Is there a time limit?
We do ask that guests respect the 2 hour time limit so that our other guests can enjoy SooShii as well!
